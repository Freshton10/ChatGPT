### Prebuilt reports

**Prebuilt Reports** (also referred to as canned reports) are reporting templates that administrators can utilize for viewing certain types of data. Some of the available templates include:
Active Contacts
Active Agents
Campaign Performance
Contact History
Evaluation Report
Skill Performance
Time Utilization
To run a report:
Sign in to your Contact Center account.
In the left menu under Data and Analytics, click Reporting. The list of Prebuilt Reports will show by default.

Click the name of a prebuilt template you want to run.
Click Run Report.
Note: Prebuilt Reports will only run successfully if relevant data is available.
