### How to Reset Cisco Deskphones ?

You can reset Cisco SPA302, SPA303, SPA501G, SPA502G, SPA504G, SPA508G, SPA509G, SPA512G, and SPA514G phones by following the steps below:

1. Press the Menu button.
 
2. Press Setting.
 
3. Navigate down to Factory Reset option.
 
4. Press select.
 
After restart, your phone will be successfully reset.
