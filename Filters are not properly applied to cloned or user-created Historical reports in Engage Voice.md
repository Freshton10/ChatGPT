### Filters are not properly applied to cloned or user-created Historical reports in Engage Voice

When viewing an existing Historical report in viewer mode, filters that you attempt to use are not applied to the report. The only filters that get applied are those that have saved, preselected values that were added to the report in edit mode.
Note: This applies only to cloned and user-created reports.

**Edit your report and apply filters in edit mode**

1. Sign in , then click on the Analytics icon.
2. Go to Historical reports > Private .
3. Click on the report you want to filter.
4. In viewer mode, click on Edit in the upper right-hand corner.
5. In edit mode, apply your filters.
6. Click Save in the upper right-hand corner.
