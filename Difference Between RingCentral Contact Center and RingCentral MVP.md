### Difference Between RingCentral Contact Center and RingCentral MVP

This article compares which needs are addressed by RingCentral Contact Center and RingCentral MVP. Click on the links below to learn more about RingCentral Contact Center and RingCentral MVP features.
Note : This article does not apply to Engage customers.
 
**What is RingCentral Contact Center?**
RingCentral Contact Center omnichannel solution lets customers contact your company on the communication channel of their choice while giving agents ready access to the data needed to solve problems quickly and efficiently. With powerful administrative tools and a simple drag-and-drop development environment, RingCentral Contact Center offers different products and plans that dramatically improve the overall call center experience.

RingCentral Contact Center product page: https://www.ringcentral.com/contact-center/overview.html
 
**What is RingCentral MVP?**
RingCentral MVP gives your business everything you need to streamline your operations with unified communications. With a cloud-based phone system, you can configure multi-level IVRs and custom call queues and departments. Empower your employees to work anywhere with their own devices using RingCentral Desktop apps, mobile apps. RingCentral MVP connects your office, remote and mobile employees under one phone system, regardless of their location.
RingCentral MVP product page :  https://www.ringcentral.com/office/how-it-works.html
