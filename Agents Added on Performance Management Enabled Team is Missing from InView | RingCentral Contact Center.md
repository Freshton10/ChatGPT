### Agents Added on Performance Management Enabled Team is Missing from InView | RingCentral Contact Center

This article provides instructions on how to resolve if the agents added to the Performance Management (inView) enabled team is missing from inView in Central.
Note:
This article does not apply to Engage customers.
Agents added to a Performance Management (inView) enabled team is not showing as Users on inView in Central.
Performance Management (inView)
Central
When a user is created in Central or moved to a Performance Management (inView) enabled team, they will appear in the appropriate spot for reporting. However, this does not create a login user.
The users must be added within the Users page of the Accounts tab of Performance Management (inView).
