### Understanding supervisor dashboards | RingCentral MVP

**Monitoring or barging in on a digital interaction**
1. In the left navigation bar, open the Supervisor menu.
2. Click on the name of the agent you want to monitor to open the agent details screen.
3. Under Digital interactions, you’ll see your agents’ active digital interactions and stats. Click the interaction you want to monitor to open the digital interaction detail view. To return to the main view, click the back arrow next to the agent’s name.

Note:
If you enter text into the text field, then click Send, your message will:
Be visible to the agent and the customer.
Appear as if it comes from the agent.
Be included in the message history.
