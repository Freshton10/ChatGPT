### WhatsApp conversations by type report

The **WhatsApp conversations** by type volume report is a stacked bar chart that shows the different types of conversations per day. Learn more about volume reports in Engage Digital.
Note: Reports use WhatsApp’s definition of conversation: "an exchange that takes place within a 24-hour period." Learn more about WhatsApp conversations pricing.
Free-tier Business-initiated conversations: Conversations initiated by a business within the free-tier quota (the first 1,000 conversations of each month).
Free-tier User-initiated conversations: Conversations initiated by an end-user within the free-tier quota (the first 1,000 conversations of each month).
Billable Business-initiated conversations: Conversations initiated by a business in excess of the free-tier quota.
Billable User-initiated conversations: Conversations initiated by an end-user in excess of the free-tier quota.
The report lists the number of conversations that are used to calculate your account’s monthly WhatsApp usage.
