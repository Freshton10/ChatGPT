### Activate or Deactivate a Skill | RingCentral Contact Center

**NOTE :** This article does not apply to Engage customers.
 
Below are the steps of activating or deactivating a skill in Central:
Log in to Central
Go to  ACD > Contact Settings > Skills
Make sure that the Show  drop down on the upper right side is set to All
Locate the Skill either by searching for the  ID Number  or  Skill Name , or just find the Skill name from the list
Click the Skill name 
On the Details tab, click on  Activate  or  Deactivate  button.
