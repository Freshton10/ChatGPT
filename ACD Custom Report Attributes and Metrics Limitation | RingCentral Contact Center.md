### ACD Custom Report Attributes and Metrics Limitation | RingCentral Contact Center

This article provides information about the maximum amount of attributes and metrics added to an ACD Custom Report via RingCentral Contact Center.
NOTE
: This article does not apply to Engage customers.
What are the maximum amount of attributes and metrics that can be added to an ACD Custom Report?
ACD Custom Reporting
In an ACD Custom Report, you can add a maximum of 10 attributes and 30 metrics per report.
