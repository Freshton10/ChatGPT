### Reporting a call quality issue
When agents encounter issues with an inbound or outbound call, they can report them to their admins.
1. Log in to Engage Voice, then click Agent.
2. In the left navigation bar, click Call history.
3. Click the phone number where you experienced the issue.
4. Click the three-dot More icon to the right of the Call details header.
5. In the dropdown, select Report call quality.
6. In the Report call quality popup window, select one of the following options:
I do not hear the customer (silence).
The customer does not hear me.
The customer’s voice is garbled/choppy.
Customer complains my voice is garbled/choppy.
Call disconnected unexpectedly.
Other
7. In the Notes field, enter any details about the issue.
8. Click Report issue. When you’ve submitted your report, you’ll see a green confirmation message in the top right corner of the page.
