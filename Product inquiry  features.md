### Product inquiry - features**

Contact Center customers may see an opening for enhancement or improvement of our products. We always appreciate feedback from our customers about how we can improve our products and services.

**Issue with existing feature**
To inquire about issues with an existing Contact Center product, please submit a ticket to our support agents.
Go to support.ringcentral.com. From the Cases tab, click Log in for more information. Enter your RingCentral login credentials.

Click Check System Status. You will see notifications that are relevant to your account and organization. The RingCentral Timeline section lists all past events for future reference and diagnosis.
Click Create New Case, located next to the RingCentral Timeline section.
Verify your contact information. Update if necessary, then submit.
Click Please select product area, then select Contact Center Solutions.

Click Please select product issue, then select the topic that most closely matches the reason for your inquiry.

Click Please select severity level, then select the urgency of your issue. Click Severity Definitions at the top right corner to learn about security levels. For Level 1 cases, please call RingCentral Support directly.

In the Subject field, enter a subject for your concern. Provide relevant details in the Description field. Click Submit when done.
To review the list of articles related to your case, click I need to see the list of articles. To create a new case, click Create case.
Verify your contact information in the popup window. You may enter an alternate email address for case updates. Click Next.
You may attach files to provide more information about your case. Click + Add, then click Choose a file in the popup window. Browse for the file, then click Upload.

You may also enter comments, if you like. Click Add comment, then enter text in the Comments field. Click Add comment when youâ€™re done.

**Feature enhancement request**
Have an idea on how we can improve our products? We would love to hear from you. To provide us with a feature enhancement request, please provide the following information:
Account BUID
Account Contact requesting the enhancement
Details of the enhancement:
What is your use case and who is the user (agent, supervisor, customer, etc.) for your request?
What problem are you trying to solve or what problems does the current functionality cause
What steps did you take to get there?
How do you want the feature/product to work (proposed solution)?
Any screenshots or data to go along with the enhancement request
